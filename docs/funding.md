## Grant Application Process

### Phase 1: Research & Preparation (4-6 weeks)

1. **Grant Research**
   - Identify relevant grants
   - Review eligibility criteria
   - Assess alignment with mission
   - Review past recipients

2. **Documentation Preparation**
   - Organizational documents
   - Financial statements
   - Project descriptions
   - Team bios

3. **Partnership Development**
   - Identify potential partners
   - Develop partnership agreements
   - Establish MOUs

### Phase 2: Proposal Development (6-8 weeks)

1. **Concept Note**
   - Executive summary
   - Problem statement
   - Solution approach
   - Expected impact

2. **Full Proposal**
   - Detailed project description
   - Budget and timeline
   - Team and partners
   - Sustainability plan
   - Monitoring and evaluation

3. **Supporting Documents**
   - Letters of support
   - Partnership agreements
   - Financial documentation
   - Legal documents

### Phase 3: Submission & Follow-up (2-4 weeks)

1. **Submission**
   - Final review
   - Submission
   - Confirmation

2. **Follow-up**
   - Acknowledgment
   - Additional information requests
   - Interview preparation (if applicable)

### Phase 4: Award & Implementation

1. **Award Notification**
   - Review terms
   - Accept award
   - Set up reporting

2. **Implementation**
   - Project kickoff
   - Regular reporting
   - Grant compliance
