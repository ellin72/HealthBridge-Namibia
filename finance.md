**Order to Team:**  
You are instructed to design and implement a secure, transparent, and user-friendly **billing system** within HealthBridge Namibia. This system must ensure that patients can pay consultation fees and service fees seamlessly, while protecting financial data against fraud and cyber threats.  

---

## üî® Build Instructions

### 1. **Payment Integration**

- Integrate trusted payment gateways (e.g., locals only).  
- Support multiple payment methods: debit/credit cards, mobile money, and bank transfers.  
- Ensure transactions are processed in real time with confirmation receipts.  

### 2. **Billing Module**

- Create a **Billing Dashboard** for patients to view invoices, receipts, and payment history.  
- Enable providers to set consultation and service fees within their profiles.  
- Automate invoice generation after each consultation or service.  

### 3. **Security & Fraud Prevention**

- Encrypt all financial data in transit and at rest.  
- Implement **two-factor authentication (2FA)** for payment approvals.  
- Maintain **audit logs** of all transactions for fraud detection.  
- Apply **PCI-DSS compliance standards** for handling card payments.  

### 4. **Transparency & Accountability**

- Patients must receive digital receipts (email/SMS/app notification) after every payment.  
- Providers must have access to a secure dashboard showing earnings and payouts.  
- Admins must be able to monitor and reconcile transactions.  

### 5. **Scalability**

- Design the billing system to support **subscription models** (monthly wellness plans, student research packages).  
- Allow for **discount codes, promotions, and bundled services** in future phases.  
- Ensure modularity so billing can expand with national and regional scaling.
